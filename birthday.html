Akan Name Calculator

Akan names are derived from the Ghanian culture.
Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name',which corresponds to the day in the week they wer born.
Here are the Ghanian day names:

Male
  • Sunday:Kwasi
  • Monday:Kwadwo
  • Tuesday:Kwabena
  • Wednesday:Kwaku
  • Thursday:Yaw
  • Friday:Kofi
  • Saturday:kwame
Female
